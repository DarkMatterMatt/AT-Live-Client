Routes:
This website provides live tracking for buses, trains and ferries on the Auckland Transport network. Track a new route by clicking the and change its color by clicking on the left of its icon in the menu bar. Remove by clicking on the right of its icon in the menu bar. Markers with a dark center are travelling in the opposite direction to those with a light center. (Dark center dots tend to move towards the city).
